#### Requirements:
* The source code
* An Arduino Uno
* MG90S 9G Metal Gear Servo Motor
* IC2 128x64 oled Display
* 3x four pronged buttons 12x12x7.3 mm
* Adafruit SSD1306 Arduino Library (Display)


#### Instructions:
###### Breadboard Wiring:
* Power (Uno) to Power (Breadboard)
* Ground (Uno) to Ground (Breadboard)
* A4 (Uno) to 3A (Breadboard)
* A5 (Uno) to 1A (Breadboard)
* Digital 2 (Uno) to D15 (Breadboard)
* Digital 3 to 20E (Breadboard)
* Button1: Ground bridges across to 15C (Breadboard)
* Button2: Ground bridges across to 20C (Breadboard)
* VCC (Display) to Power (Breadboard)
* Ground (Display) to Ground (Breadboard)
* SCL (Display) to 1B (Breadboard)
* SDA (Display) to 3B (Breadboard)

#### Notes:
* Positive on a Breadboard is power
* Negative on a breadboard is ground
